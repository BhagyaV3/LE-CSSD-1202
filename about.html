About Us

What is an Ecological Footprint?

The ecological footprint is a model used to quantify the consumption and
demands placed on local natural resources by communities and the people in them.
(It should not be confused with the carbon footprint as the ecological footprint
involves natrual resourses other than just carbon).

What is ECOfooTracker?

ECOfooTracker is a free, online, application where users can track and optimize
their ecological footprints, therefore being more mindful of their daily
activities in support of our environments and many of the living beings in them!
